Adding Notes to Bookmarks — During Live Meeting

How should the note input appear when bookmarking during an active meeting?

A

Inline Toast with Input

Bookmark toast becomes interactive — shows a text input. Type a note and press Enter, or dismiss to skip. Auto-dismisses after 5s if ignored.

🔖 Bookmark at 12m 34s

Pro: Minimal disruption — appears in toast area. Con: Small input, easy to miss.

B

Two-Step: Bookmark First, Note Later

Ctrl+B or click instantly creates bookmark (toast confirms). To add a note, open the bookmark panel (toggle button in header) and edit there.

C

Hybrid: Toast + Quick Note + Panel

Toast has a small "+ Note" button. Click it to expand into an input. Ignore it to save without note. Full panel still available for later editing.
