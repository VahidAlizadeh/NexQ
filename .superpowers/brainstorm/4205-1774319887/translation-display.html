How should translations appear in the transcript?

Consider readability during a fast-paced meeting — you need to glance and understand quickly

You I think we should focus on the Q2 roadmap first
Creo que deberíamos enfocarnos primero en la hoja de ruta del Q2
Them Agreed, the timeline is tight and we need to prioritize
De acuerdo, el plazo es ajustado y necesitamos priorizar
You Let me share my screen with the project board
Déjame compartir mi pantalla con el tablero del proyecto

A — Inline Below

Translation appears directly under each line in muted italic. Always visible when enabled. Clean hierarchy — original is dominant, translation is secondary.

B — Side-by-Side Columns

Original on left, translation on right separated by a subtle divider. Both visible at once. Compact vertical space but requires wider window.

C — Hover Tooltip

Transcript looks clean by default. Hover over any line to see its translation in a floating tooltip. Zero visual clutter but requires mouse interaction.

You I think we should focus on the Q2 roadmap first
Them De acuerdo, el plazo es ajustado y necesitamos priorizar ES
You Let me share my screen with the project board
Click the language badge to toggle between original ↔ translation

D — Replace with Toggle

Translation replaces the original text. A small language badge lets you click to swap back. Cleanest look but you can't see both at once.

Note

This is for full-line auto-translation display. The word/phrase lookup (select-to-translate) will always use a floating tooltip regardless of this choice.
